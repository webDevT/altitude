Why Altitude?

Altitude is not your normal AD network partner. We are focused on providing you with real opportunities to monetise your traffic, no matter it's source.

Altitude is focused on easily integrating with your apps and games, to become a core partner within your AD waterfall, all whilst providing you with a real opportunity to monetise non-IOS and non-Google Play traffic effectively.

Altitude supports all inventory types, standard banners, interstitials and rewarded videos to newer formats such as Audio as well.
